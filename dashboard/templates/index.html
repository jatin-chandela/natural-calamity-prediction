About This System
CNN + LSTM Model

A hybrid Conv1D + LSTM architecture processes 30-day meteorological windows. The CNN layers extract local patterns; the LSTM layers capture temporal dependencies to produce flood and cyclone probability estimates.

Federated Privacy

Raw sensor data (rainfall, temperature, wind speed, humidity, pressure) never leaves each city node. Only model weights are shared with the central aggregator via Federated Averaging (FedAvg).

Risk Prediction

The global model predicts flood and cyclone probability per city, averaged across the 7 most recent 30-day windows. Risk levels are colour-coded: low (<30%), medium (30–60%), and high (>60%).
